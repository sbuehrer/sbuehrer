About Me


My name is Scott Buehrer and I am an IT Technician for the City of Bryan. I am a techie professionally and by nature. I maintain company servers, storage, and security.
I am also responsible for keeping the employees tech needs working. I am available for contract work on home network, small business network systems (including wireless),
and pretty much anything technology. When I am not working on something nerdy I like to go fishing, preferably from a boat.
I am not a very good fisherman by any means, but I do enjoy it. Some of my favorite places to fish and type of fish are:

  • Turkey Foot Creek - Crappie
  • Auglaize River - Crappie
  • Devils Lake - Blue Gill
  • Cold Water Lake - Bluegill and Bass
  • Jacksonville Beach - too many to list


If you are need of any technical service, repairs, or you just want to go fishing, please contact me.
